Mission Statement

The Black River Fullbackers Club is a charitable, tax-exempt 501(C)(3) organization whose members are committed to work for the improvement of the Black River Football program. The Fullbackers Club focuses its funding of the program to create the safest competitive program possible. The fundraising is a "grass roots" community effort that relies strongly on volunteer efforts to make all of its events successful.

Fullbacker Events

Reverse Raffle

Held in April and Oct. at the Lorain County Fairgrounds, the reverse raffle is our largest event.

  • $4,000 grand prize
  • Prime rib dinner by Smith's Catering
  • Beverage bar
  • Variety of raffles and instant tickets

Brat Fest

Held in January and March at the Lorain County Fairgrounds, this event offers a bratwurst dinner, drinks, music, and games for a much needed break from the winter weather.

Al 'Buz' Anderson Memorial 5K Run

Held in June at Firestone Park Black River High School in Spencer, this event also offers a one mile walk and an awards ceremony after the race.
