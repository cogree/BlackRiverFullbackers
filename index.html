Mission Statement

The Black River Fullbackers Club is a charitable, tax-exempt 501(C)(3) organization whose members are committed to work for the improvement of the Black River Football program. The Fullbackers Club focuses its funding of the program to create the safest competitive program possible. The fundraising is a "grass roots" community effort that relies strongly on volunteer efforts to make all of its events successful.
